# AMAs

> Reach AMAs are live, online events to support founders on operational needs and market trends. Convening on Fridays, these sessions invite thought leaders, operators and industry experts from across the education, technology and business sectors to discuss topics pertinent to your needs and growth.

As a founder, you will automatically receive email updates and calendar invitations to upcoming AMAs. You can also invite your colleagues to [receive updates and invitations here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSf8p3XG2eNz_An9qnZhq6zz3kgQwAV4ww8vjEsZJNabamhSBw/viewform).
