Introduction (00:02 - 01:11)
James introduces Jimmy, the founder and CEO of Guided Growth Marketing, highlighting his extensive experience in demand generation and edtech.
Demand Generation Overview (01:11 - 06:15)
Jimmy defines demand generation as the marketing process to drive awareness and interest in a company's products or services. He discusses strategies for engaging prospects, including events, webinars, and content marketing. The importance of aligning sales and marketing teams is emphasized, advocating for a unified go-to-market approach.
Implementation of Demand Generation (06:15 - 10:30)
Jimmy compares traditional lead generation (casting a wide net) to account-based marketing (ABM) (fishing with spears), focusing on targeted accounts. He explains the tiering of accounts (Tier 1, 2, and 3) and how to allocate resources accordingly.
Lead Generation Strategies (10:30 - 13:20)
Key components of lead generation campaigns include email marketing, website overlays, paid media, and webinars. Jimmy notes the effectiveness of LinkedIn for targeting specific audiences, particularly in the education sector.
Account-Based Marketing (13:20 - 17:05)
ABM is highlighted as a strategy that can lead to significant revenue increases by focusing on high-value accounts. Jimmy discusses the importance of personalized messaging and targeted content for different stages of the buyer's journey.
Planning and Execution (17:05 - 27:01)
Emphasizes the need for collaboration between sales and marketing teams to align goals and strategies. Discusses the importance of measuring success through key performance indicators (KPIs) related to revenue and opportunity creation.
Questions and Answers (27:01 - 58:10)
- The importance of buy-in between marketing and sales teams.

- The role of email marketing and how to effectively engage prospects.
