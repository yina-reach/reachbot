What kind of videos can VAAS create?
Editing, motion design, product demos, social clips, explainers, and custom animation. If it involves post-production, we can handle it. We also can shoot on-site. Ask us about it!
How does the process work?
One kickoff call, then you share assets via cloud folder. We set up your dedicated project board and Slack channel. Communication happens through timestamped video comments and your project board. No endless meetings. One project at a time. Here's why that's a feature. We work on one project at a time per client (two in Studio). It's how we keep quality high and turnaround predictable. If you have multiple videos lined up, we sequence them and keep momentum constant.
What's your turnaround time?
Depends on scope. Simple edits move fast. Complex animations involve scripting, storyboarding, and design frames first. We work on one project at a time to maintain quality, and set expectations up front so there are no surprises.
What makes VAAS different?
Every video starts from what your brand is trying to say and who needs to hear it, then gets built to move a real number. The design and motion are the easy part. Knowing what the video needs to do is the actual work, and it's where most shops stop short.
Who owns the final videos?
You do. We reserve the right to use work in our portfolio unless otherwise agreed. We respect NDAs and white-label arrangements. Your confidential projects stay confidential.
How do you use AI?
We built an internal AI operations layer that handles briefs, ideation, scheduling, and tracking. Our editors spend their time on creative work instead of admin. You get a partner that brings you video ideas before you ask, and output that stays consistent month after month.
