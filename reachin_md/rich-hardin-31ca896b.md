**Bio:** Rich is a self-funded health plan specialist with nearly a decade of experience building and operating third-party administrator (TPA) solutions. As President of Edison Health Solutions, he oversees a full-service TPA dedicated to patient-first plan design: covering claims adjudication, PBM, care navigation, and utilization management. He previously served as EVP at ResourceOne Administrators, where he led client services and benefit operations for employer groups across the country. He's also a board member of SPBA (Society of Professional Benefit Administrators), where he created and leads a program for emerging industry professionals.
Rich is a strong resource for founders navigating the self-funded employer market. Particularly on TPA operations, plan design, benefits solutions, and how payers and administrators actually interact at the ground level.
