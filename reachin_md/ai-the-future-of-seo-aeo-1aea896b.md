02:55 - Trends in AI and User Behavior
Marcos outlines the significant increase in referral traffic from AI-driven platforms, noting a shift in user behavior from traditional search queries to more conversational interactions with AI tools. He emphasizes that users are now asking longer, more detailed questions, which reflects a change in how people seek information.
09:43 - Transition from Keywords to Questions
The conversation shifts to the transition from traditional keyword research to "question research." Marcos explains that marketers need to focus on understanding the questions users are asking LLMs rather than just the keywords they are typing into search engines. This shift requires a new approach to content creation and SEO strategies.
18:29 - Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)
Marcos introduces the concept of Answer Engine Optimization (AEO), which combines elements of traditional SEO with the new dynamics introduced by AI. He discusses how businesses can optimize their content to appear in AI-generated answers and the importance of citation optimization, which involves being referenced as a reputable source in AI responses.
22:01 - Tracking and Measuring Performance
Marcos explains the necessity of tracking performance across different AI models and how to measure a brand's presence in AI-generated answers. He suggests using tools to analyze how often a brand appears in responses to relevant questions and emphasizes the importance of understanding variations in user queries.
28:59 - Content Strategies for Optimization
The discussion covers strategies for optimizing owned and earned media. Marcos advises on the importance of high-quality content and how it can influence AI responses. He also discusses the role of technical SEO in ensuring that content is accessible to AI models.
37:10 - User-Generated Content and Its Impact
The conversation touches on the role of user-generated content (UGC) in AI search. Marcos notes that platforms like Reddit and Quora are favored by AI models for their text-based content, while visual platforms like Instagram have less impact due to accessibility issues.
50:03 - Longevity of Strategies and AI Content
Marcos addresses concerns about the longevity of current strategies and the use of AI-generated content. He shares insights from a study indicating that human-created content performs better than pure AI content in SEO rankings, recommending a balanced approach that combines human creativity with AI assistance.

The AI Search Revolution
- LLMs are rapidly absorbing traditional SEO traffic, evidenced by prominent cases like Chegg, HubSpot, and WebMD.
- AI platforms like ChatGPT attract hundreds of millions of users, leading to a significant and growing source of referral traffic for publishers.
- User behavior is shifting from iterative keyword queries on Google to single, conversational questions/prompts on AI chat products (e.g., ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google SGE, Bing Copilot).
- The focus of SEO is evolving from "keyword research" to "question research" or "prompt research" to understand deeper user intent.
How AI Search Works (RAG)
- AI models function by predicting the "next word," acting as highly advanced auto-completion tools.
- Their output is profoundly influenced by context, including explicit instructions, user preferences, and retrieved external information.
- Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) is a core technology: AI systems pair an LLM with a search engine to run queries, retrieve relevant text "chunks" from web pages, then synthesize answers while citing sources.
- RAG helps LLMs overcome common issues like hallucinations and lack of recent data, improving the accuracy and reliability of their responses.
AEO: Optimizing for Answer Engines
- "Answer Engines" represent the new hybrid search landscape, blending traditional search with generative AI capabilities (e.g., Google SGE, Bing Copilot, ChatGPT with browse).
- This shift necessitates Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) as the successor to traditional SEO.
- AEO focuses on two key surfaces: 1) **Answer Optimization** (influencing what the AI model says about your company or topic directly in its response) and 2) **Citation Optimization** (driving traffic to your website by being cited as a reputable source within AI answers).
Influencing AI Answers (Mentions)
- **Track Across Models:** Monitor your brand's presence and perception across various AI models (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity), as each yields different responses.
- **Analyze Question Variants:** Small changes in user prompts or added context can significantly alter AI responses; track these variations relevant to your target audience.
- **Measure Share of Answers:** Quantify how often your product or brand appears in AI responses for your target questions (e.g., "Trello appears 38% of the time").
- **Niche Down Strategically:** In competitive markets, optimize for highly specific, differentiated long-tail questions (e.g., "best payroll management software for international contractors") to gain initial visibility.
Driving Traffic via Citations
- **Technical SEO Foundation:** Strong technical SEO is non-negotiable; if your site doesn't rank on traditional search engines (Google, Bing, Brave), it won't be cited by AI models.
- **Server-Side Rendering:** Ensure your website content is server-side rendered, as AI models primarily process plain text effectively.
- **Earned Media & PR:** Proactively seek mentions and coverage on authoritative publishers that rank for queries AI models might generate internally. Value these mentions higher due to their amplified reach via AI.
- **Affiliate Arbitrage:** Companies with established affiliate marketing programs often achieve greater AI visibility due to widespread brand mentions in training data.
- **Leverage UGC:** Optimize for user-generated content platforms like Reddit and Quora, as AI models increasingly favor these sources for opinions and discussions (e.g., Perplexity's "social mode").
Content Strategy for AEO
- **Comprehensive Content:** Create in-depth content that explicitly addresses potential AI model needs for sourcing claims, such as dedicated sections on pricing, features, or compliance.
- **Structure for Citations:** Design content to make it easy for AI models to extract specific, verifiable information to back up their answers (e.g., a "website cost" page with clear breakdowns by size and complexity).
- **Utilize "Writing Assistant" Tools:** Employ SEO writing assistants (e.g., Surfer, Clearscope, Graphite's tool) to identify content gaps and patterns from top-ranking sites, ensuring your content is comprehensive.
- **Continuous Optimization:** Regularly refine landing pages that receive AI-driven traffic, adapting content to align with what models want to say and cite based on observed patterns.
Measuring AEO Impact
- **Traffic Tracking:** Use referrer data (e.g., a free dashboard Marcos offers) to monitor direct traffic from AI models to your site and identify high-performing landing pages.
- **User Research is Key:** Due to limited data from AI platforms, implement post-purchase surveys and lead form questions ("How did you hear about us?") to capture AI-driven conversions.
- **Deep Dive into User Journeys:** Conduct user interviews to understand specific queries, modifiers, and decision-making processes involving AI tools that led them to your brand.
- **Monitor Negative Sentiment:** Actively track and manage mentions on UGC platforms (Reddit, Quora) to mitigate potential brand damage from AI-amplified negative sentiment.
