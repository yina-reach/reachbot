02:55 - Trends in AI and User Behavior
Marcos outlines the significant increase in referral traffic from AI-driven platforms, noting a shift in user behavior from traditional search queries to more conversational interactions with AI tools. He emphasizes that users are now asking longer, more detailed questions, which reflects a change in how people seek information.
09:43 - Transition from Keywords to Questions
The conversation shifts to the transition from traditional keyword research to "question research." Marcos explains that marketers need to focus on understanding the questions users are asking LLMs rather than just the keywords they are typing into search engines. This shift requires a new approach to content creation and SEO strategies.
18:29 - Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)
Marcos introduces the concept of Answer Engine Optimization (AEO), which combines elements of traditional SEO with the new dynamics introduced by AI. He discusses how businesses can optimize their content to appear in AI-generated answers and the importance of citation optimization, which involves being referenced as a reputable source in AI responses.
22:01 - Tracking and Measuring Performance
Marcos explains the necessity of tracking performance across different AI models and how to measure a brand's presence in AI-generated answers. He suggests using tools to analyze how often a brand appears in responses to relevant questions and emphasizes the importance of understanding variations in user queries.
28:59 - Content Strategies for Optimization
The discussion covers strategies for optimizing owned and earned media. Marcos advises on the importance of high-quality content and how it can influence AI responses. He also discusses the role of technical SEO in ensuring that content is accessible to AI models.
37:10 - User-Generated Content and Its Impact
The conversation touches on the role of user-generated content (UGC) in AI search. Marcos notes that platforms like Reddit and Quora are favored by AI models for their text-based content, while visual platforms like Instagram have less impact due to accessibility issues.
50:03 - Longevity of Strategies and AI Content
Marcos addresses concerns about the longevity of current strategies and the use of AI-generated content. He shares insights from a study indicating that human-created content performs better than pure AI content in SEO rankings, recommending a balanced approach that combines human creativity with AI assistance.
