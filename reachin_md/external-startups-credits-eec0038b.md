Who is eligible for Vercel for Startups?
Applications are evaluated using the following criteria:
- Startup must be affiliated with an approved Startups Partner (see full dropdown list above). This typically includes companies in accelerators, those backed by Venture Capital firms, or startups that meet certain criteria set by Vercel.
- Startup must not have previously received Vercel for Startups credits.
- Startup must have raised a Series A or less, and must apply within 12 months from their most recent funding round.
- Startup must have a company website, and an email address that matches the company website.
- Startup must have proof (screenshot of partner dashboard, hub, wiki, or console) demonstrating affiliation with an approved Vercel for Startups partner.
What are Vercel Credits?
Vercel Credits are a fixed discount that can be used towards your Vercel usage, including seats, faster data transfer, compute, and more. These credits allow you to use Vercel's pro features without immediate cost, helping you to scale your startup efficiently.
Vercel Credits are only available on the Pro and Pro Trial plans. Credits are automatically subtracted from your monthly subscription fees. Total credits issued are divided into equal monthly increments. For example, $30,000 in credits would be issued as $2,500 credits/month for 12 months. Unused credits do not roll over to the next month.
Do Vercel Credits apply to v0?
v0 is a separate product line, so Vercel Credits cannot currently be applied to v0. We are exploring how to bring v0 credits to the Startup program, too!

- Opportunities for featured marketing by Vercel and Vercel’s leaders on social media.

Where can I view my credits?
To view current bill:
- Navigate to your Team's Dashboard in Vercel
- Select "Settings"
- Select "Billing"
You should see a $0.00 bill. Clicking "View Charges" will give you a detailed breakdown.
To view your past invoices:
- Navigate to your Team's Dashboard in Vercel
- Select "Settings"
- Select "Invoices"
You should see a "Discount" badge next to invoices that have Vercel Credits applied.
Please note, you will not be able to view discounts while on a Pro Trial. They will be visible once your account converts to a Pro account. Also, the Vercel Dashboard's upcoming invoice UI currently has an issue where it is not aware of discounts that start in the next period, even though they affect the upcoming invoice. We apologize for the lack of visibility and insight this provides, and we are working on a fix.
