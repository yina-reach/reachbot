Frequently asked questions
Who can apply?
Any startup that has raised up to $10M in equity funding, has fewer than 50 employees, and is associated with one of our Startup Partners listed here. Existing customers are not eligible.
What if I'm not affiliated with any of the above partners?
If your startup is not associated with an approved partner, we encourage you to ask your accelerator, investors, or incubator to apply to our Startup Partner Program here!
What can I do with Superhuman Mail's Business Plan?
Our Business Plan includes our full productivity suite, plus our most advanced AI (Auto Drafts, Ask AI, Custom Auto Labels), Hubspot and Salesforce Integrations, and Recent Opens.
How do I join as a Startup Partner?
We want to hear from you! Apply to be a partner and we'll get back to you with more information.
