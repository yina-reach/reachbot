# Prakash Patel

**Current Title:** Managing Partner at Kairos Growth Services
**LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/prakash-patel-m-d-health/
**Advisory Group:** 🫀 Healthcare
**Areas of Expertise:** Operations, Behavioral health, Value-based care models, Sustainable business models
**Bio:** Prakash is a physician-operator with experience spanning clinical training, startup formation, and large-scale healthcare leadership. He trained in orthopedic surgery before helping spin out a half-dozen pharma and device companies, then spent years running major health organizations. At GuideWell he served as COO and President, helping scale the company past $20B in revenue. At Elevance (formerly Anthem), he grew the Diversified Business Group 6X in three years and led a behavioral health transformation from under 1% digital to over 30%.

He's currently building in cardiology and focuses on value-based specialty care: high-cost, chronic, longitudinal conditions he sees as the next frontier of healthcare reform.

His framework for evaluating solutions: is it material, meaningful, measurable, and manageable? He's a strong resource for founders who want a rigorous operational perspective on payer dynamics, care delivery models, and scaling within complex health systems.
