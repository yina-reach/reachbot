Parchment Growth Acceleration Through M&A
- Parchment executed three strategic acquisitions to accelerate growth
  - Vertical integration for existing customers drove significant expansion
  - 2018: zero diploma customers → 2025: 30% of US higher ed institutions use Parchment for diplomas
- Digitary acquisition
  - Expanded international footprint beyond US to meet growth-stage expectations: ~10% revenue from outside primary geography
  - Significant product overlap but accretive to revenue
- Quotele acquisition added dual enrollment and core sharing solutions
  - Connected platform in meaningful way
  - Expanded product portfolio strategically
Partnership Strategy with Competitors
- Education market loves partnerships and these can be key growth levers
- “Frenemy” relationships valuable and not a zero-sum game if executed thoughtfully
- Best practices:
  - Establish MOUs before RFPs arrive (“put roof on when sunny, not raining”)
  - Make primary/secondary decisions upfront
  - Keep two-sided networks open and level playing field
  - Build relationships with competitor CEOs at industry events
Sales Team Restructuring and Territory Optimization
- 2017: Hard look at Parchment sales team revealed territory gaps
  - Average deals per rep and deal values analyzed
  - “Nobody’s been to Idaho in two years” - untapped markets identified
- Split territories smaller, added more reps
  - Accretive to overall growth despite individual pressure on CAC
  - Series C/almost D stage, cash flow positive when implemented
- Recommendation: Always evaluate “can 3 reps generate more than 2?”
  - Front-load investment but ensure enablement and implementation can handle volume
Sales Productivity Frameworks
- Sales Velocity formula (tracked weekly):
  - (# opportunities × average first year contract value × win rate) ÷ average days to close
  - Sliced by sector (K12, Higher Ed, international), rep maturity, individual performance
  - Weekly rewards for best sales velocity across all roles
  - Smooths out quarterly “gas pedal” sales patterns
- Deal Banding analysis:
  - Win rates by deal size bands (e.g., <$1K, $1-10K, $10-25K, etc.)
  - 6-9 month historical win rate data by band
  - Pro forma forecasting 6 months out
  - Helps identify BS deals (e.g., department never closed 6-figure deal)
Sales Team Structure and Hiring
- Split K12 and Higher Ed sales leadership
  - Different motions: K12 = low ACV/high transaction, Higher Ed = high ACV/fewer deals
  - Geographic management problematic when mixing deal types
- SDR team recommendations:
  - Build outbound SDR team as soon as financially viable
  - Train on methodology, move to AE roles within 6-12 months
  - Avoid hiring “closers” - hasn’t worked in Kevin’s decade of experience
- Hiring considerations:
  - Former educators better suited for customer success vs. sales
  - Match deal complexity to rep experience (SMB vs. enterprise)
  - First 2-3 head of sales hires usually wrong (too junior/senior)
  - Figure out: do you want a super rep, or a team builder?
Performance Management and Enablement
- “If it’s not in Salesforce, it doesn’t exist” - non-negotiable rule
- Demo certifications required:
  - Annual recertification for all reps (including veterans)
  - Grade on specific criteria, must achieve “B” minimum
  - Product changes require updated certifications
- Pipeline tracking essentials:
  - Track creation date and qualification requirements
  - Monitor activities leading to pipeline creation
  - Analyze close-loss patterns by stage for product/pricing insights
- Sales rep core functions: create pipeline, manage pipeline, close pipeline
  - Most reps excel at 2/3 functions, unicorns do all three
  - Senior reps often resist prospecting
Leadership Philosophy
- Best sales reps are the most selfish, and best sales leaders are the most selfless — important to identify which is which for your sales hires
- Caution against promoting super rep to sales leader without behavioral change
- “No breath is better than bad breath” - make hard personnel decisions early
- Focus reps only on activities that produce results
