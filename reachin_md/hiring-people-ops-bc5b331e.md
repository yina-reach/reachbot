# Training & Workshop Providers
### Leadership and new managers
- [Hone](https://www.google.com/url?q=https%3A%2F%2Fhonehq.com%2F&sa=D&sntz=1&usg=AFQjCNFsdyi9cmv3Pmz_kJQrDAuqBmIoNQ) (a Reach portfolio company) offers an all-in-one leadership development training platform for distributed teams
- [People Runway](https://www.google.com/url?q=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.peoplerunway.team%2F&sa=D&sntz=1&usg=AFQjCNHtmFExLL-9zZmuApC5qkLsmQPMXQ) offers 1:1 coaching, consulting, and workshops to help startups scale their people operations and intentionally build high-functioning organizations (Candice Reimers, creator of Reach's HR Playbook, is a co-founder of this org.)
- [Woven HR](https://www.google.com/url?q=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.wovenhr.com%2F&sa=D&sntz=1&usg=AFQjCNEYIRuLVEClUELQNVf4wfWWqMZ3qg) offers a range of leadership training, coaching, talent operations, and sexual harassment training
