# Ednition

Ednition provides infrastructure-as-a-service tools to K-12 edtech companies for rostering, single sign-on, LMS integrations, and secure data syncing. Its platform, RosterStream, gives edtech companies the infrastructure they need to integrate with districts  – without getting bogged down in custom builds, complex data transformation, or expensive internal infrastructure projects.
