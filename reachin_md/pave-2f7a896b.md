# Pave

What's [Pave](http://pave.com/marketdata/reach-capital)?
Reach Capital companies have access to Pave’s real-time compensation data from 8,700+ companies to benchmark pay for 250+ job families across 50+ countries and 90+ cities and metros.
Pave uses automated and persistent connections to HRIS, ATS, and EMS platforms to collect salary and equity data in real-time. Plus, advanced AI automatically matches your people to Pave's job architecture.
Pave Offers the Following Partner-Exclusive Benefits
- Free access to Market Data Lite, Pave’s overall U.S. market benchmarks + one additional market. Usually limited to companies under 200 employees but accessible to all Reach Capital companies.
- 20% off to Market Data Pro, Pave's all-access dataset, minimum savings of $1,800 USD/year. At purchase, use code PAVEPARTNER2026
- Get started at [pave.com/marketdata/reach-capital](http://pave.com/marketdata/reach-capital)
