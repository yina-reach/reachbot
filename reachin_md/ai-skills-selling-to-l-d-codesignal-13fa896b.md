# AI, Skills & Selling to L&D - CodeSignal

- https://share.fireflies.ai/embed/meetings/F2h04gZGveRgV0Yo
Introduction and Background (00:03 - 01:47)
- Tigran is the co-founder and CEO of CodeSignal, a leading skills platform focused on assessing and developing skills for individuals and teams.
- CodeSignal initially concentrated on coding assessments but is expanding into soft skills.
Founding Journey and Business Evolution (01:47 - 08:08)
- Tigran started CodeSignal to address the "talent problem" he identified while working at Google, where qualified candidates were often overlooked based on their resumes.
- The company has evolved from focusing solely on assessments to integrating educational components.
- CodeSignal is currently post-Series C, having raised significant funding to scale its operations.
Current Market Context and Demand for Skills (08:08 - 13:15)
- Despite recent layoffs, the demand for CodeSignal assessments has increased, with more candidates competing for fewer roles.
- Tigran believes that the need for skilled developers will grow, especially as AI technologies simplify software development.
Advice for Recent Graduates (13:15 - 15:10)
- Tigran advises recent graduates to adopt a growth mindset and continuously improve their skills.
- He emphasizes that the bar for employability has risen, necessitating better education and skill development.
Transitioning CodeSignal From Assessments Back to Learning (15:10 - 21:50)
- CodeSignal is integrating learning components into its offerings, addressing unmet needs in the educational market.
- The company aims to provide personalized learning experiences and effective assessment tools.
Challenges and Strategies in Selling to L&D (21:50 - 39:14)
- Tigran discusses the challenges of selling to Learning and Development (L&D) departments, which often view learning solutions as optional.
- He highlights the importance of demonstrating measurable skill gains to prove the value of learning products.
Soft Skills Assessment (39:14 - 49:00)
- Tigran explains the challenges of assessing soft skills and how CodeSignal uses simulations and AI to provide realistic assessment experiences.
- The company has developed an AI tutor, Cosmo, to give real-time feedback on soft skills assessments.
AI Integration and Future Outlook (49:00 - 54:10)
- The conversation touches on the role of AI in enhancing educational experiences and the need for collaboration in the edtech space.
- Tigran expresses optimism about the potential for innovation in education and the importance of building a community of learners.
