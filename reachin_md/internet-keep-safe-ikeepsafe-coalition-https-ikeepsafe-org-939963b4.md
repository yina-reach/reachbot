# [Internet Keep Safe (iKeepSafe) Coalition](https://ikeepsafe.org/)

**Format:** Website
**Files & media:** https://ikeepsafe.org/
**Area:** Privacy
**Description:** Offers service that certifies digital products as compliant with state and federal requirements for handling protected personal information. They also help organizations achieve and maintain compliance through product assessments, monthly monitoring, annual training, and assistance with remediation.
