**Speaker:** [Manuel Bronstein](https://www.linkedin.com/in/manuelbronstein/)

Intro & Current Role at Roblox (01:17 - 04:12)
- Manuel discusses his responsibilities at Roblox, which include partnerships and business development.
- His three main areas of focus: collaboration with the CEO on vision and strategy, involvement in product development processes, and improving organizational capabilities.
Product Management Philosophy (04:13 - 12:19)
- Manuel reflects on the balance between data-driven decision-making and intuition in product management.
- He shares experiences from his time at Xbox and Zynga, illustrating how intuition can guide long-term vision while data informs short-term decisions.
Challenges and Convictions (12:20 - 16:01)
- He recounts a project at Xbox where he faced pushback on a creative idea but persisted by addressing concerns and finding solutions.
- Emphasizes the importance of conviction in product leadership.
Balancing Short-Term vs. Long-Term Goals (16:02 - 20:00)
- The importance of balancing short-term goals with long-term vision in product strategy. Describes Roblox’s approach to quarterly guidance and how they prioritize long-term projects while still addressing immediate needs.
Engaging Non-Technical Leaders (20:01 - 24:09)
- Insights on how product leaders can effectively engage with non-technical leaders by fostering empathy and understanding between teams. Emphasizes the importance of communication and collaboration across functions.
Common Mistakes in Product Leadership (24:10 - 27:00)
- One common mistake: accepting compromises too easily during product development. Encourages product leaders to push for better solutions and to understand the underlying issues in decision-making.
Structuring Product Teams (27:01 - 33:06)
- Discusses the evolution of product organizations as companies scale, including ratios of product managers to engineers.
- Highlights the importance of generalist roles in early-stage companies and the need for specialization as the company grows.
Hiring a Chief Product Officer (33:07 - 38:28)
- Outlines scenarios for hiring a CPO, emphasizing the need for clarity on the role's expectations.
- Difference between hiring for execution versus visionary leadership.
Voice of the Customer (38:29 - 41:58)
- Manuel explains how Roblox channels customer feedback and emphasizes the importance of addressing user pain points.
- Discusses the role of anecdotes and direct engagement with users in shaping product decisions.
Remote Work Policy (41:59 - 46:09)
- Manuel shares insights into Roblox's decision to transition back to the office, emphasizing the importance of in-person collaboration for innovation. Describes the hybrid model implemented to balance remote work with office presence.
Community and Safety (46:10 - 55:16)
- Roblox’s approach to fostering user creativity while ensuring platform safety, particularly for younger audiences. Highlights the use of AI and human moderation to manage user-generated content and interactions.
Balancing Roadmap Priorities (55:17 - 59:34)
- Manuel discusses the framework for designing product roadmaps, balancing customer requests, visionary projects, and infrastructure needs.
- Emphasizes the fluidity of resource allocation based on the company's current demands.
