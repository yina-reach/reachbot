Emma Kate Fittes is a staff writer for EdWeek Market Brief covering the K-12 marketplace, including trends in curriculum, instruction, professional development, school finance, and district purchasing. Her beat focuses on the implications of K-12 policy and practice for education companies. She has covered K-12 for more than a decade, including statewide education issues and policy for Chalkbeat Indiana and suburban districts for the Indianapolis Star.
