# SaaS: Human Capital Technology - Talent Development (2016)

A look at industry drivers in talent development and technologies targeted at corporate L&D. Covers LMS, content management systems, video-based content providers, MOOCS, career-prep providers. assessment/analytics/credentialing, and social/gamified learning.
