## What you’ll find in ReachIn
ReachIn is a portal to the people, networks and resources across the extended Reach community to help our founders scale and succeed. Here you’ll find:
Expert Directories
  - Consultants and support providers in sales, marketing, product, hiring, legal and other business needs.
  - Executive and leadership coaches to help founders grow and establish foundations for success.
  - Media networks and journalists who shape public opinion and share our founders’ work and impact.
Market Research & Industry Benchmarks
  - KPIs and benchmark metrics from our portfolio and across the broader tech industry.
  - Latest market reports from investment banks and industry analysts commissioned by Reach.
  - Reach market deep dives into education sub-sectors including Web3, the Creator Economy and Work-Integrated Learning.
Community & Networking Events
  - Virtual AMAs with experts who help our founders navigate the education and technology markets.
  - Founders’ Day, our annual gathering for founders and the extended Reach community
  - In-Person Workshops facilitated by industry experts who built Google, Netflix and other market leaders.
  - Founders’ Dinners to recharge, rejuvenate and celebrate the good times.

Playbooks and Guides
  - Operational matters from board management to legal, compliance and HR needs.
  - Growth and scaling including sales, marketing, customer success and recruiting talent.
  - Product development and measuring its efficacy and impact.
