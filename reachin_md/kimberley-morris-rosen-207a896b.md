# Kimberley Morris Rosen

About
[Kimberley Morris Rosen](http://www.linkedin.com/in/kimberley-morris-rosen-318b3319) is a lawyer with over 13 years of experience at the intersection of law, education, and innovation. Kimberley has served as special counsel at Emerson Collective and in-house counsel at AltSchool, and has supported early- and growth-stage organizations such as The Primary School. She now leads her own practice, [KMR Law](https://thekmrlawfirm.com/), partnering with education and edtech organizations as outside or fractional general counsel, as well as on individual matters.
In addition to her legal practice, Kimberley is an elected trustee on her local [school board](https://www.pvsd.net/about/governing-board). She brings a practical, collaborative approach to legal work — covering contracts, employment, governance, product, compliance, and strategic advising — with deep knowledge of the education space. For organizations seeking legal or strategic support, she welcomes inquiries at [kimberley@thekmrlawfirm.com](mailto:kimberley@thekmrlawfirm.com).
