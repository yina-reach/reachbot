# How it works
The ElevenLabs Startup Grants program is open to startups and small companies with fewer than 25 employees, anywhere in the world.
To apply, you’ll need to share a few details about how you plan to use ElevenLabs, whether it's for a game, an education tool, a support bot, or something entirely new.
> For ElevenLabs’ startup partnership offerings (including credits), founders can apply through this link: [https://elevenlabs.io/startup-grants](https://elevenlabs.io/startup-grants). Please have them select ReachCapital as the referrer and use the code ReachCapital11 when applying.

Please note! Per their [FAQs](/1faa896bb9b68088b72fe584cced536b), ElevenLabs is unable to award grants to companies that are building projects for children aged 18 or under.
If accepted, your ElevenLabs account will be upgraded immediately. You’ll receive 33 million characters of audio generation credits to use over the next 12 months, completely free.
There are no strings attached. Whether you need a few voice lines or millions of words, this gives you the freedom to build without worrying about cost.
If you reach the 33M character limit early, you can talk to us about scaling or upgrade self-serve to a paid plan. For most early-stage projects, it’s more than enough to get started.
At the end of the grant, you can move to an Enterprise plan, switch to a standard paid plan, or pause your usage entirely. It’s commitment-free beyond the 12-month term.
