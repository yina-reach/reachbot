Introduction and Overview of Edtech Conferences (00:00 - 01:15)
Frank Catalano talks about the 180 Edtech conferences listed on the Reach website, providing a public service to the ed tech ecosystem. Most of these conferences are national and state events.

Frank Catalano Introduction and Tips (01:26 - 03:08)
Frank, a seasoned edtech marketing executive, introduces himself and shares his background.
He emphasizes the importance of clear communication at conferences, specifically in exhibit booths, to make it easy for attendees to quickly understand who you are and what you do.

Founders and Executives Sharing Marketing Strategies (03:09 - 13:52)
Founders and executives from different companies share their marketing strategies and thoughts about their 2024 conference calendars. Maria Barrera (Clayful), Devlin Daley (Derivita), Rami Joseph (Stellic) and Jarah Euston (WorkWhile) discuss strategies like focusing on local and regional events, leveraging speaking opportunities, and balancing between quantity and quality of conferences.

Speaking Opportunities and Pay-to-Play Events (13:53 - 33:31)
The discussion moves to the topic of securing speaking opportunities at conferences and the trend of "pay-to-play" events. The participants discuss the value and potential pitfalls of these events, with insights from both the organizers' and attendees' perspectives.

Identifying Local and Regional Events (33:32 - 39:50)
The participants discuss strategies for identifying local and regional events that may be more targeted and beneficial. Suggestions include talking to customers, using online resources, and leveraging relationships with conference organizers.

Post-Conference Follow-Ups (39:51 - 49:27)
The conversation shifts to the importance of following up after conferences. Participants share best practices, including the need for timely, personalized follow-ups and the importance of segmenting the follow-up process based on the level of interest shown by the leads.

Conference Hacks and Conclusion (49:28 - 57:12)
The participants share their favorite "conference hacks" for making a memorable impact at conferences.
