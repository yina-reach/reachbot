01:12 Behavioral Science Overview: The difference between hypothetical decisions and actual behavior, the ways in which our choices are influenced by our environment, and how removing friction leads to powerful change in user behavior.
05:01 Irrational Labs’ 3B Framework:
1. Identify Key Behavior: Identifying key behaviors that organizations want to encourage, and understanding specific actions related to desired outcomes, is crucial for effective product design. This involves breaking down high-level goals into actionable steps that can be measured and optimized.
1. (11:48) Reduce Barriers: Shares insights from their work with One Medical, where they identified logistical and psychological barriers that prevented users from booking preventive healthcare appointments.
1. (19:49) Amplify Benefits: How to make future benefits more immediate and concrete to encourage user engagement. Examples include using clear messaging and tangible rewards to motivate users, such as showing immediate benefits of actions like brushing teeth or using a financial service.
26:05 Other Real-World Applications and Case Studies:
Drawing from their work with TikTok and Google AdWords, illustrating how behavioral insights can lead to significant improvements in user engagement and decision-making processes by addressing frictions and enhancing the perceived benefits of products
