# OpenAI

### OpenAI <> Reach Capital Partnership
The mission of the OpenAI Startups Team is to help startups harness the power of OpenAI’s
platform to build exciting new products and experiences using AI. We want to make sure you
have all the resources you need to build and scale. Below are some helpful resources for
getting started and tapping into the expertise of the OpenAI team.

> $10k in OpenAI API Credits
Your portfolio companies can apply for OpenAI credits by following the instructions below:
  1. Sign up for an OpenAI account [here](https://platform.openai.com/signup) and enter your billing details (required) [here](https://platform.openai.com/settings/organization/billing/overview)
  1. [Apply for credits here](https://q.surveyfeedbackresponse.com/jfe/form/SV_bjzNK0KeYKdAfGK) and enter the email associated with your OpenAI account
  1. Enter the unique referral code: reachcap_2025
Note: Startups will need to meet the eligibility criteria listed on the form and not have raised
Series B or later funding. Credits are processed weekly. If a company has received credits
previously from OpenAI, we'll grant the difference between $10k and the previous amount.
Credits are only applicable to API usage and cannot be applied to ChatGPT usage.

Upgrade to Usage Tier 5
Have your portfolio companies shoot us an email at [startups@openai.com](mailto:startups@openai.com) to have their
account upgraded to Usage Tier 5, which unlocks top rate limits and access to more models.
Technical Support & Events
We would be happy to schedule a day of office hour sessions for your portfolio companies,
either in person or virtual. We would also be happy to participate in AI days with your portfolio
companies. Please reach out to us when interested.
Email Alias and Direct Contact
Have your portfolio companies shoot us questions, blockers or escalations at
[startups@openai.com](mailto:startups@openai.com). Any contacts at Reach can reach out directly to [cevans@openai.com](mailto:cevans@openai.com).
Join OpenAI’s Startup Community
Gain access to monthly technical talks, upcoming events, and top AI resources that will help
