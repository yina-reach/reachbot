Jessica Guynn is a veteran national correspondent and senior reporter on the consumer and money team with nearly 40 years of journalism experience.
She joined USA TODAY in 2014 after stints with the Los Angeles Times, the San Francisco Chronicle and Knight Ridder newspapers where she worked as a technology correspondent, a business reporter and columnist and as an investigative reporter.
At USA TODAY, she covers business, politics and culture, workplace and diversity and social media and technology.
Our journalists adhere to the USA TODAY NETWORK Principles of Ethical Conduct For Newsrooms.
