ABOUT JIM
Jim Marsden has spent decades guiding leaders through the terrain where personal growth and organizational performance converge.
With a background spanning engineering and physics, executive leadership at Hewlett Packard, and twenty years leading transformative wilderness experiences, Jim brings an uncommon combination of technical precision, business strategy and management, and deep human wisdom to the work.
He's not a coach who stays on the surface, or a consultant who hands you a deck and leaves. He's a guide for the journey that matters most — the one that changes how you lead, how your organization performs, and who you're becoming along the way.
The setting may be different from time to time — a boardroom, a mountainside, a week in the wilderness, a one-on-one call at the edge of something new. What stays constant is what emerges: clarity about what matters, the conviction to act on it, and a quality of leadership that holds up long after Jim has gone.
