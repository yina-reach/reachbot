PVSD Governing Board of Trustees
Serving as a Trustee on the school board position is an impactful way to contribute to the Portola Valley School District community and shape the future of education. Your involvement can help address pressing issues such as curriculum standards, budget allocations, and school safety, all while promoting transparency and accountability within the district. We invite you to bring your unique perspective to the table to continue the important work that benefits the next generation of Portola Valley students and makes a lasting difference in the community.
The PVSD Governance Team has developed a Governance Handbook, which:
- Defines PVSD’s mission, governance structure, and community-funded budget model.
- Outlines trustee roles, ethics, and governance norms.
- Details meeting protocols and Brown Act compliance.
- Clarifies board-superintendent responsibilities and evaluation.
- Highlights community engagement, policies, and key bylaws.
