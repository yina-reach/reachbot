Introduction & GenAI Implementation (00:11) Pedro discusses the method of implementing GenAI into CoachHub specifically through the use of ChatGPT, and particularly how it can be prompted to act as a content manager for marketing campaigns.

Specific Use Cases (06:41) Pedro goes into detail about how GenAI can be used in specific business cases. For example, it can be used to create email content for different customer segments. Pedro also discusses how AI and data are being integrated within CoachHub.

Operations Automation (22:21) Pedro shares his experiences with operations automation, discussing how chatbots and other AI tools can be used to automate repetitive tasks.

Culture and Team Allocation (28:32) Pedro discusses the importance of fostering a culture that embraces AI and how to allocate team resources effectively for implementing GenAI within a company. He suggests starting small with chat prompting and gradually assigning more resources as the relevancy of the technology becomes apparent.
Measuring Impact and Cost of GenAI (38:00) Pedro talks about taking costs into consideration with using GenAI as a core offering of your product; for example, 30 minutes of conversation with a big large language model can costs $5. He also discusses how to measure the impact of GenAI from the lens of user engagement and feedback.

Training and Up-skilling (46:59) Pedro highlights the importance of training and up-skilling employees to effectively use GenAI tools and technologies within their workflows, emphasizing the need for clear guidelines and principles.

Centralized vs Decentralized Approach (49:09) Pedro discusses the decision-making process between a centralized and decentralized approach to using GenAI, emphasizing the need for a balance between the two based on specific use cases and business objectives.
