# ReachIn Connect — Approver Guide (for Tony)

You're the approver for **ReachIn Connect**, the inbox that auto-files resources
(PDFs and links people email in) into the right ReachIn / Notion database.
Your whole job is to reply to a proposal email with one word.

---

## What you'll receive

Emails from **reachin@reachcapital.com** with a subject like:

> **ReachIn Connect: review — \<title\>**

Each one shows a **proposed Notion entry** — which database it would go into, plus
the drafted title, description, and tags.

## What you do — reply with one of these on the first line

| Reply | What happens |
|-------|--------------|
| `APPROVE` | Files the entry exactly as shown |
| `REJECT` | Discards it — nothing is saved |
| `USE: <database>` | Files it into a different database instead |
| `APPROVE` + field lines | Edits fields first, then files (see below) |

**Databases you can redirect to with `USE:`**
Library Database · Good Reads · Marketing & Branding · Consultants & Coaches ·
Reach Advisors · Media Contacts · Session Recordings

**To edit before filing**, put `APPROVE` then the fields to change, one per line:

```
APPROVE
Area: Fundraising
Tags: fundraising, markets
```

## That's it

- Just type the word at the top of your reply and send — no need to delete the
  quoted email text.
- Within an hour it files into Notion and you get a **"✅ filed here: \<link\>"**
  confirmation.

## Good to know

- **Only your replies** can file things — it checks your email address, so no one
  else can approve.
- It **only accepts submissions from `@reachcapital.com`** senders; outside or
  automated email is ignored.
- Anyone at Reach submits by emailing a **PDF (attached)** or a **link (in the body)**
  to `reachin@reachcapital.com` — no special subject needed.
